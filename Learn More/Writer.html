A technical writer creates and maintains documentation, such as user manuals, guides, and technical specifications, to communicate complex information in a clear and understandable manner. They collaborate with subject matter experts to gather and organize technical content, ensuring accuracy, consistency, and adherence to industry standards. Technical writers also conduct research, write, edit, and format documents to meet the needs of the target audience. Strong communication, writing, and organizational skills are essential for success in this role, as well as the ability to work independently and meet project deadlines.
